01 — Studio

The name is the method.

Tvashtr is the divine craftsman of Vedic myth — the one who forged the tools the other gods fought with. That is the brief this studio holds itself to.

Tvaṣṭr
त्वष्टृ — "the carver, the shaper, the fashioner"

In Vedic mythology, Tvashtr is the celestial architect who forged Indra's Vajra and shaped the forms of all living things — joint by joint, with obsessive care. His craft was never decoration; every object had to work.

Every site, edit and script here is built the same way — as an instrument, not decoration. The practice runs lean by design: one founder, full-stack across design, code, video and copy — so every project keeps a single point of view from brief to launch.

03 — Vision

Why Tvashtr exists.

Tvashtr started with a simple frustration: most creative work for small brands feels borrowed — a template with new colors, a logo that could belong to anyone. The vision behind this studio was to build things that feel genuinely your own, not assembled from someone else's kit.

That means designing around what a brand actually needs and what its people actually want to see — then holding the execution to a standard that doesn't cut corners. Own it, want it, and make it top-notch — that's the whole brief, every time.
