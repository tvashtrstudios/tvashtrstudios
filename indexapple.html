About Apple Hospital

Trusted treatment, modern technology, compassionate care.

Apple Hospital is a leading multispeciality hospital in Indore, offering advanced medical services, an expert team of doctors, and 24×7 emergency services. We specialise in AI-assisted knee replacement (PIXEE), complex surgery, and full-body health check-ups — approved under Ayushman Bharat and all major Mediclaim networks.

🎯 Our Mission

Comprehensive, accessible care that strengthens community health, with a team that puts patients first in every interaction.

🔭 Our Vision

To set the benchmark in patient satisfaction and clinical outcomes as Indore's most trusted name in health.
